Frequently Asked Questions

What is fractional ownership?

Fractional ownership allows multiple investors to own a share of a high-value property, making real estate investment more accessible and affordable.

How do I earn returns?

You earn returns through regular rental income distributions and potential capital appreciation when the property value increases.

Is my investment secure?

Yes, your investment is secured by real property. We also implement strict legal and financial measures to protect our investors' interests.

Can I sell my fraction?

Yes, you can sell your fraction on our platform, subject to certain conditions and availability of buyers.
