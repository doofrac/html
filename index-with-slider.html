Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our fractional real estate investment platform.

Fractional real estate ownership allows multiple investors to own a portion of a property. This approach makes it possible to invest in high-value properties with a smaller capital outlay, diversify your portfolio, and share both the benefits and risks of property ownership.

Doofrac ensures the security of your investment through various measures, including thorough due diligence on all properties, transparent documentation, secure payment gateways, and compliance with all relevant regulations. We also work with reputable partners and maintain insurance coverage to protect against unforeseen circumstances.

The minimum investment amount varies depending on the specific property and the fraction size available. Generally, our platform allows you to start investing with as little as ₹50,000. This low entry point enables more investors to participate in the real estate market and build a diversified portfolio.

Investors can earn returns through two primary methods: rental income and capital appreciation. Rental income is distributed proportionally to investors based on their ownership stake. Capital appreciation occurs when the property value increases over time, potentially resulting in profits when the property is sold or when you sell your fraction.
